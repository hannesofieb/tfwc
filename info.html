The Fictional WIne Club Manifesto

Welcome to The Melbourne Chapter, where the romantics within us all are passionate experimenters. Here, spirits soar and rules break. With every glass raised, we make a stand—for life, for passion, for wine—and pledge ourselves to live fully, deeply, wildly, without regret.

We gather not just to drink but to live deep and suck out the marrow of life. We cast aside the chains of wine snobbery, those rigid rules of formality that have made wine culture a distant, exclusive world. Here, we celebrate wine as it should be: raw, free, alive. Whether it’s a rich red or a crisp white, a daring orange or a playful rosé—these are what we stay alive for.

At The Fictional Wine Club, we challenge the traditions that confine us. Inspired by Melbourne’s carefree, open-hearted spirit, this club embraces curiosity, adventure, and the enchantment of the moment. We rebel against the idea that wine must fit into preordained boxes, instead encouraging free drinkers to explore, taste, and celebrate wine on their own terms.

Drink deeply. Live boldly. And when we’re old and grey, we’ll know we truly lived.
